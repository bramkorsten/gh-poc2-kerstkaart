About

Merry Christmas!

Welcome to the Greenhouse 2019 Christmas game! This game is a way for us to show you everything that's possible using modern technologies. To do this, we made you a game of Rock, Paper, Scissors, using only the web! There is even a hidden feature for some users, to experience WebAR, the future of AR applications.

How to play?

To start playing the game, click the door icon in the top right! Go find an opponent to play against and try to set the best highscore!

There are three options in Rock, Paper, Scissors. You can probably guess what the options are...

Rock beats Scissors

Paper beats Rock

Scissors beats Paper

About AR Mode

The AR team within Greenhouse is working very hard to create the most elaborated AR experiences. New technologies play a huge part in this! That is why we are looking into the new area of AR called WebAR. This technology enables the use of AR without any apps, just by using your browser! This means you don't have to download a new app to experience this AR app.

Unfortunately, since this technology is quite new, right now, it only works on relatively new Android phones. To try this out, please download Google Chrome Beta on your Android device. Then, open this website again and look for the icon in the top bar!

Freeroam
